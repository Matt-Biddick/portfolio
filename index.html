About

Hello there! I'm Matt, a New Zealand raised evolutionary ecologist based near Munich. I wake up in the morning, grab a cup of coffee, open up my laptop and write scientific stories that people will remember.

Over the years I have developed a tried-and-true system for writing captivating papers fast. Many have suggested I share this knowledge to help aspiring researchers to do the same.

So, I'm offering one-on-one coaching packages where I help you turn your hard work in the field and laboratory into phenomenal publications.
